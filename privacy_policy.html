Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 19, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Watermelon Todo handles user data.

1. Data Collection

Watermelon Todo does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All user data remains private on the user's device and iCloud (if enabled).

2. Permissions

  • Microphone: Used for voice input. No recordings are stored or shared.
  • Calendar: Used to integrate tasks with the user's calendar.
  • iCloud Storage: Used for syncing tasks across devices, managed by Apple's iCloud service.

3. Security

We prioritize user privacy by ensuring no personal data is processed externally. The app does not have analytics tracking or third-party data collection.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at dev@feioustudio.com.

By using Watermelon Todo, you acknowledge that you have read and agreed to this Privacy Policy.
